Quiet night with friends

Sun, Jan 12, 2003

I didn't get off my ass and create my own system yet so I'm still using Radio. As long as I keep things simple I only have to shut it down once per entry.  I did do a little bit of work on my RSS Aggregator.  It is starting to look like a real program.  Sometime over the next week Dare and I are going to get together and merge codebases.

Gerhard and Angela came over tonight and we made dinner.  We did a recipe out of the Alton Brown cookbook.  I really like this book and Alton's show, Good Eats.  We did the "Chicken Piccata" on page 170 and it turned out pretty good.  For desert we made a something from one of his shows: Spicy Pineapple Sauce.  That turned out well also.

The spicy pineapple sauce calls for a finely minced habanero pepper.  I did the mincing and about an hour latter I rubbed my eye.  The searing pain could only be stopped with prolonged dunking of my head under the sink.  Where is one of those eye wash things from chemistry class when you need them?  I've learned my lesson -- always wear gloves when dealing with hot peppers.
